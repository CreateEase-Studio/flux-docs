Data Deletion Request

At CreateEase Studio, we value your privacy. Since Flux Files operates primarily as a local file manager, we do not store your personal files on our servers.

How to request data deletion:

If you wish to delete any data associated with your use of the app (such as purchase tokens or crash reports), please send an email to: createease.studio@gmail.com

What data will be deleted:

  • In-app purchase history linked to our internal analytics (if any).
  • Technical identifiers used for crash reporting (Firebase/Crashlytics).
  • Advertising identifiers (AdMob).

Your request will be processed within 7 business days. Please note that some transaction data must be retained by Google Play for legal and financial compliance.
